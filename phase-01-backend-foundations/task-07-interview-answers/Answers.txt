Section 4: Databases (SQL)


14. What is a primary key?
    unique identifier for every single row in database.

15. What is a foreign key?
    A column in one table that links directly to the Primary Key of another table.it make relationships between two tables 

16. What is a one-to-many relationship?
    one single record in Table A can be linked to multiple records in Table B


17. Why do we use JOIN? 
    atabases separate data into different tables to avoid duplication, we use JOIN to temporarily glue those tables back together in our queries

18. What is the difference between table and entity?
    A "Table" is the physical grid of rows and columns. An "Entity" is the C# class.
